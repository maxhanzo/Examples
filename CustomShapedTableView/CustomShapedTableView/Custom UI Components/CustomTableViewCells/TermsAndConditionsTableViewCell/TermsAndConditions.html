The AWS Customer Agreement is the legally binding agreement that Amazon wants you to read and agree to in order to set up an account.

In your case, this can be a Terms and Conditions, a Terms of Service, a Terms of Use, a Privacy Policy or a Website Disclaimer that users must read and agree to in order to create an account on your web site or mobile app.

The same checkbox method should apply to already registered users of your website, mobile app, SaaS app, Facebook app etc.

If you already have registered users and you’re planning an update to the legal agreements, you should provide a notice to users of the changes before the effective date of the agreement.

You can implement the clickwrap method, with the “I Agree” checkbox, in cases where you are updating your Terms agreement or Privacy Policy agreement and you want to notify users about these updates in order to read and accept the new terms.

This is how Elance shows a notification on their updated Terms agreement to their registered users.
